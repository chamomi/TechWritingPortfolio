How to Receive a Payment from the Customer

To process a payment from a customer made with a bank transfer, you need to:

  1. Open Accounting/Receive Payment form
  2. Select the customer you got the funds from
  3. Select the payment currency
  4. Enter the unique payment ID
  5. Enter the payment date
  6. Enter the amount of money you received
  7. Select the payment method that was used
  8. Enter the payment destination as provided by your customer
  9. Select the orders to be paid with the funds (fully or partially)
  10. For each order that will be covered by this payment, enter the amount of the funds to be allocated to the order.
    If the payment is not fully allocated, the remaining funds are added to the customer’s balance and will appear in the Pre-payments wallet in the     Sales Balance section.
  11. Click Apply to confirm the allocation
